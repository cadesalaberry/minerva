uPrint - Password Waiver for McGill ID cards

  

This form lets you choose whether or not you wish to be prompted to enter your McGill password every time you swipe your ID Card at a Xerox uPrint device. By default, McGill faculty and staff members are not prompted for their password, while students and other members of the McGill community are. For added security, it is recommended that you do enable the password prompt by selecting PSWD Not Waived.

If you have more than 1 McGill ID card listed, it is because you have multiple statuses at the university, e.g. you are a student and a staff member.

Even if you decide to disable the password prompt, you still have to enter it if you log in by manually entering your McGill Username or Short Username. Return to this form at any time to cancel the waiver.

Any change you make here will only take effect after the next data update period. These occur daily at 10am, 1pm, 4pm and 6pm.

For more information on uPrint, please see:

  • uPrint - Campus Printing for Students
  • uPrint - Campus Printing for Faculty and Staff

Note: This form has no impact on any other services / login methods apart from using your McGill ID card to login to a uPrint device.

Acknowledgment and Authorization

Should you choose to waive the password prompt after swiping your McGill ID card at a uPrint device, please keep in mind that:

  • If your card is stolen or used by another individual, you are responsible for any charges made; they will not be refunded.
  • You should never lend anyone your McGill ID card. Once logged in with your ID card or credentials, any scans sent from a Xerox uPrint device will appear to have been sent by you.

Note: If your McGill ID card is stolen or lost, contact Security immediately at 398-3000 have your card deactivated.
